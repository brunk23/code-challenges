The object of this game is to be the last person to take any of the stones.
Rules
  1. The game will start with three piles of random amounts.
  2. No two piles will be allowed to start with the same amount of stones.
  3. A starting player (student or teacher) will be randomly picked.
  4. You must take at least one stone.
  5. You may only take stones from one pile in a turn.
  6. You may take as many stones as you link, including all the stones in a pile.
The teacher will play automatically. The text will show his move before the numbers update.
